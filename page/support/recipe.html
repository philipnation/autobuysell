What Is Recipe?

Recipe is Finology's wealth-building platform that includes tools to checkup your financial profile, goal calculators, dedicated stock research services and personalised investment advisory services.

What is your investment philosophy?

At the core, we prioritise capital safety in our investment analysis and recommendations. We recommend a stock only if it passes our in-house DEEPSCAN test, wherein we check if the company has the following characteristics-


D- Discount to intrinsic value (Reasonable Valuations)
E- Efficiency of business operations (Cost Advantage)
E- Ethical Conduct
P- Pricing Power (Strong Moat)
S- Scalability (Visibility of Earnings growth)
C- Competitive Landscape (Industry Projections)
A- Appropriate Safety Net (Risk Mitigation)
N- No Biases (Behavioral Safeguard)

Who selects the stocks and other financial products that are recommended or covered in reports?

Finology Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is a SEBI registered investment advisor. Our research analysts are actively involved in the stock-picking & filtration process.

Finology 30 is our flagship stock research service designed for long-term investors. Here's what all you get in Finology 30:

 

  • 30 Stocks for the long-term
  • Buy it Below price
  • Detailed Research Reports

  • Premium articles
How can I add or change my financial inputs?

You can add or change your financial inputs in the Financial Checkup section. The ''Check'' button can be used to add your financial inputs and ''Recheck'' for editing them.

What is Financial Checkup?

Financial Checkup section in Recipe has a bunch of free tools that allow you to input your financial details and check what's missing. It includes checkup of your savings, investments, emergency, insurance, debt & risk profile.

What do you mean by Risk Profile?

Risk Profile helps you identify the type of investor you are based on your capacity and willigness to take risks. Identifying your investor type helps you to build a portfolio that suits you.

Will you recommend mutual funds and stocks for me based on my risk profile?

Recipe’s purpose is to enable you to check, analyse and fix financial issues by yourself. And so as a DIY investment platform, you will only get algorithm-based standard solutions. The risk profile identified is not used to filter the stocks. The stock recommendations and mutual fund reports are common for all and have been picked based on their suitability for most use cases. However, their suitability to your risk profile and goals has to be determined by yourself.

Will you manage my Portfolio?

We do not offer portfolio management services under Finology 30 or Finology ONE. Instead, our services will help you invest for creating wealth in the long term. With our Financial Checkup tools, Goal calculators and Stocks, you can start managing your portfolio by yourself.

 

However, if you need an expert to design an investment plan based on your risk profile, you can apply to our investment advisory service 'Reserve.'

Which service should I take for a short-term investment?

All our stock recommendations under Finology 30 are for long-term investments. However, we cover important debt instruments like liquid funds, fixed deposits, etc, in the Free Reports section.

What is Emergency Fund?

An emergency fund is a cash reserve that's specifically set aside for unplanned expenses or financial emergencies. Some common examples include car repairs, home repairs, medical bills, or a loss of income.

 

You can use the Emergency Fund tool under Recipe's Financial Checkup section to check if your emergency fund is adequate or not.

What is the Debt tool?

Debt tool helps users scan their debt status and identifies whether the user is at the risk of running into a debt trap. It considers both the running EMIs as well as total loans to check the debt status.
