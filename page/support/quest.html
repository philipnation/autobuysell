How does Quest work?

Sign in to your Finology account to access a free course and kickstart your learning journey. You can begin in two ways: by following our Race Tracks or taking the Financial IQ quiz. Race Tracks are uniquely designed learning paths that guide you through courses based on your needs. Alternatively, the Financial IQ quiz helps assess your current knowledge and suggests courses to boost your skills. For full access to all courses, subscribe to Finology One.

What does Quest provide, and how will that help me?

Quest offers simplified, well-researched courses on finance and investing, designed to suit your learning pace. Whether you want quick insights or in-depth knowledge, you’re in control. Each course includes downloadable notes, flashcards for easy revision, and assessments to test your understanding—ensuring you not only learn but truly master the concepts.

What is Sprint by Quest?

Sprint courses are quick, bite-sized lessons on Quest, designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less. Perfect for learning on the go, they deliver essential knowledge in a short time without compromising depth.

What is Marathon by Quest?

Marathon courses are in-depth, long-form lessons designed to solidify your financial fundamentals. Combining detailed written content with video lectures, they offer a comprehensive learning experience for deeper understanding.

On the Quest homepage, our personalized recommendation tool suggests courses by answering just two simple questions. How does Quizzes offered by Quest help me?

Quest offers three engaging quizzes that not only make learning fun but also help you assess your financial knowledge. Based on your results, you'll get personalized course recommendations to further enhance your skills.

What are Race Tracks?

Race Tracks are curated learning paths that guide you through the correct sequence of courses based on your learning goals. Quest offers four distinct Race Tracks, each tailored to different aspects of investing: Academy of Value Investing, Essentials of Investing, Level Up Your Investing, and Industry Tour. Each path is uniquely designed to deepen your financial expertise step by step.

Why should I use a Race Track?

Using a Race Track offers several benefits:


1. A Guide for Beginners: If you’re new to finance, starting can be overwhelming. Race Tracks provide a clear learning path, outlining the right sequence of courses tailored to your needs.

 

2. Structured Learning: Avoid the pitfalls of jumping into advanced courses without a solid foundation. Race Tracks ensure a structured environment where you can learn step by step, mastering concepts without missing anything.

 

3. Time Efficiency: Taking on courses that are too advanced can lead to confusion and wasted time on research. Race Tracks save you time by covering essential concepts first, allowing you to upgrade your knowledge progressively.
