Get all the support related to subscription here. Start with finding your query.

What are the subscription options available through Finology?

Finology offers three distinct services.These include:

 

1)FinologyONE that offers Financial Learning and Investment Planning Tools;

2) Finology30 that offers 30 Long-Term Stocks;

3) Reserve our personalised investment advisory service.

What is Finology ONE?

Finology ONE is a subscription-based basket of three premium services: Quest, Ticker and Recipe's Premium Articles under a single plan of Rs. 599/- per month and at a discounted price of Rs. 5999/- per year. 

What is the price of Finology ONE? Is it GST inclusive?

Finology ONE is a subscription-based platform that can be accessed with a subscription plan of Rs. 599/- per month or just Rs. 5999/- per annum, both inclusive of GST.

How to use Finology ONE?

The ideal way to use the Finology ONE platform is as follows:

 

Step 1- Clear your investing concepts with the comprehensive courses on “Quest”.

 

Step 2- Practically apply what you’ve learned on Quest and evaluate the fundamentals of your stock ideas on “Ticker”.

 

Step 3- Find out our opinion on popular or trending stocks and know why we don't suggest you to invest in them with Recipe's "Premium Articles".

Is there any restriction or age limit for using Finology ONE?

No, there is absolutely no restriction or age limit for using Finology ONE.

Do I need any particular device or software of any kind to access the tools or platform of Finology ONE?

No, the system has been designed keeping in mind the various devices and platforms used to access the internet. You can use any device or browser of your choice and convenience.

What is Finology 30?

Finology 30 is our flagship stock research service where we pick 30 good stocks for long-term investors.

What does Finology 30 offer?

Finology 30 offers 30 stocks for long-term, along with their maximum buying price, detailed Research Reports, important updates and premium research articles.

Does Finology 30 come under the Finology ONE subscription?

Finology ONE and Finology 30 are distinct services and operate independently of each other.

Is it necessary to subscribe to Finology ONE to access Finology 30?

No, subscribing to Finology ONE is not a prerequisite for accessing Finology 30. We believe in providing flexibility to our users, and you can directly subscribe to Finology 30 without any additional requirements.

What is the price of Finology 30?

The price of Finology 30 is Rs.11,999/-, inclusive of GST.

Will I get all the stock recommendations at once?

No, you'll get the 30 stock recommendations on a periodic manner over the year, with 1 stock every 12 days. Subscribers will be duly notified about it in the 'Notifications' section as well as via email.
 

We want to ensure a disciplined investment approach and also enable investors extract the benefits of market dips. Hence, we don't suggest you to invest all your money in stocks all at once.

Do you provide buy and sell alerts for the stocks included in Finology 30?

Yes, we provide buy and sell alerts for all the stocks recommended under Finology 30 in the 'Notifications' section and via email. Stay informed and make timely decisions with these alerts.

What kind of stocks will it include? Will it contain large, medium, or small-cap stocks?

It will be a diverse mix, including large, medium, and small-cap stocks. This variety ensures a balanced portfolio within Finology 30, catering to different investment preferences.

Does Finology 30 also include mutual funds?

No, Finology 30 does not include mutual funds in its paid offerings. However, we analyse mutual funds and publish free reports on the ones that fit our investment ideology. You can find research reports on mutual funds, ELSS, insurance, FD, etc in the 'Free Reports' section.

What will be the validity of this service?

The validity of Finology 30 will be for one year, providing you with access to our stock recommendations, reports, updates and articles throughout this duration.

If I do not want to continue my subscription will you still provide me buy and sell alerts?

To receive all the buy and sell alerts, it's essential to maintain an active subscription to Finology 30. If you choose not to continue your subscription, access to these alerts will no longer be available.

On what basis will you select the stocks for Finology 30?

Finology 30 stocks are meticulously selected based on years of research using our DEEPSCAN framework and various criteria like market leaders, quality business, decent growth, healthy financials, management pedigree, reasonable valuation and much more.

Are there any requirements I must meet before subscribing to Finology 30?

No, there are no specific requirements to meet before subscribing to Finology 30. You can simply subscribe if you wish to embark on a long-term investment journey in the stock market.

Will I get Research Reports for the stocks available under Finology 30?

Yes, you will receive Research Reports for all the stocks available under Finology 30. Stay informed and make well-founded decisions with comprehensive rationale provided for each stock.

Will you manage the asset allocation under Finology 30?

Finology 30 is not an advisory service, and it does not include allocation or investment plans. It shall contain standard recommendations irrespective of your risk profile and goals. This list of stocks has been derived from our extensive research over the years, and is so created to suit most types of investors.

How can I access the list of stocks included in Finology 30?

You need to have an active subscription of Finology 30 to access the list of stocks.

What is Finology Reserve?

Reserve is Finology's investment advisory service where you get personalised allocation and recommendations based on your risk profile, along with a relationship manager to assist you.

What is the price for Reserve?

The service charge is Rs.30,000/- for 6 months.

What is the validity of the Reserve service?

The validity of the service is for a duration of 6 months.

Do I have to pay the fees on a percentage basis?

No, you do not have to pay the fees on a percentage basis.      

What is the Eligibility to avail of Finology Reserve service?

You only need to have some fresh capital to be eligible for Reserve. However, we prefer clients who have fresh capital of at least Rs. 10 lakh and plan to stay invested for at least 5 years. 

When will I get the investment advice in Reserve?

Once the risk profiling, payment of fees and agreement signing are done, we will provide the investment advice within 7 days.

Do I need to give you money to manage my portfolio?

No, you do not have to give us the money. Our team will provide recommendations and you have to execute the advisory from your end.

How much return can I expect from Reserve?

Reserve is a personalised investment advisory service designed to suit your needs. The returns delivered won’t be standard but rather in line with your risk profile.
