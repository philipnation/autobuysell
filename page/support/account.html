What is the best way to contact you if I have questions regarding my existing orders?

For any issue or concern related to your existing order, you can mail us at support@finology.in.

Can I use Finology ONE subscription through mobile?

Yes, the system has been designed keeping in mind the various devices and platforms used to access the internet. You can use any device (including mobile, laptops) of your choice and convenience.  

How to signup with Finology/ How to create an account?

To create an account with Finology, follow these simple steps:

 

Step 1- Kindly visit the "Login" page

Step 2- Input your Name, Email ID, and Mobile Number in the respective column and then click on the ‘Sign up’ button

Step 3- Enter the OTP received

Step 4- Set up and confirm your password

Step 5- Click on Login to Finology

 

Voila! You have successfully signed up.

What to do if I am not receiving OTP?

We send OTP to the registered mobile number and Email address. You can use either one of them. Also, don't forget to check your spam folder (just in case). A common cause for this could be an improper network and different country codes. Make sure you have a good and stable connection.

Is there any criteria for setting a password?

There are no such criteria to set a password; it can be a combination of any letter, number, and symbol.

Can I login from multiple devices?

For ease, we provide access from mobile, laptop, tablet, and other devices simultaneously. But, we would not suggest you to access from multiple devices for safety/privacy reasons.

I forgot my Password. How to login?

No problem. You can easily reset your password by following the below steps:

 

Step 1- Kindly visit this link

Step 2- Click on “Reset Password”

Step 3- Enter your registered email ID/mobile number

Step 4- You will receive an OTP on your mobile number/email ID

Step 5- Enter the OTP

Step 6- Reset the password of your choice

How can I update my Finology profile?

To update your profile, follow these simple steps:

 

Step 1- Login to your account

Step 2- Click on the “Profile” section from the left corner of your dashboard.

Step 3- Update the necessary changes as desired

Step 4- Click on “ Submit changes”

Step 5- Log out and then log in again for the change.
